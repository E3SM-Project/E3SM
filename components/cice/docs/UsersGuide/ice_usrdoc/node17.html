Example Namelists

This section shows several examples of namelists from the coupled ice model. These examples are taken directly from cice.buildnml.csh for the coupled model. Most of the variables in the namelist are determined from environment variables set elsewhere in the scripts. Since the namelists from the coupled model are "resolved" by the scripts, meaning that the values of most of the shell script variables are put directly into the namelist, examples are shown for the most commonly used configurations. Variables that are commonly changed directly in the namelist are the timestep dt and the number of subcycles per timestep in the ice dynamics ndte.
